## Nuclear Extensions

### DOE DMSP Reporting for Community Corpus Promotion

Community corpus content derived from DOE-funded research requires DMSP reporting and PID assignment upon promotion to community scope. The promotion pipeline must tag DOE-funded contributions with `funding_source` metadata so that OSTI reporting and DataCite DOI assignment can be triggered automatically. Non-research operational knowledge (e.g., facility procedures, operator notes) is exempt from DMSP reporting. See [prd-doe-data-management.md](prd-doe-data-management.md).
